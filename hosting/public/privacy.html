Worknet Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026 · Orbitra Tech

Worknet (“we”, “our”) is a local services marketplace operated by Orbitra Tech. This policy explains what information we collect when you use the Worknet mobile app, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, profile photo, and authentication identifiers when you sign up with email, Google, or Sign in with Apple.
  • Worker verification: phone number, national identity card (NIC) number for workers who publish a profile, and verification status. NIC is stored in a private account record and is not shown on public listings.
  • Location: approximate location when you allow it, to show nearby workers or set your service area.
  • User content: worker bios, photos, work samples, reviews, ratings, and saved workers.
  • Device and usage: device type, platform, session metadata, and app activity needed to operate and secure the service.

How we use information

  • Provide account, search, listings, reviews, and worker onboarding features
  • Verify worker identity and reduce fraud
  • Respond to support requests and content reports
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve reliability

Third-party services

We use Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage), Google Maps (location features), and Google Sign-In where enabled. These providers process data according to their own policies.

Sharing

Public worker profiles, reviews, and photos you publish are visible to other users. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information when required by law or to protect users and the platform.

Payments

Worknet does not process payments in the app. Any payment for services happens directly between customers and workers outside the app.

Retention and deletion

We retain data while your account is active. You may delete your account in Settings & Account. When you delete your account, we remove your profile and private verification data. Reviews you posted may be anonymized rather than deleted so listings remain useful to other users.

Your rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information in the app. Contact us to request additional help with privacy questions.

Children

Worknet is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
